Tomato Soup Recipe

From azcentral.com

Information

  • Quick & easy

Ingredients

Ingredient # of Units Unit
butter 4 tablespoon
yellow onion 1 -
minced garlic 3 cloves
celery seed 1/2 teaspoon
whole peeled tomato 2 -
chicken broth / chicken stock 1 cup
fresh basil 1* tablespoon
granulated sugar 1 tablespoon
whipping cream 1/2 tablespoon
grated parmesan cheese 1/4* cup
salt & black pepper - **

* more if needed for serving

** personal preference

Procedure

  1. Melt the butter in a nonreactive saucepan, stock pot, or Dutch oven over medium heat. When the butter is melted, add the onion and cook for 5 to 7 minutes or until very soft (we're not going for deep color here).
  2. Add the garlic and celery seed and cook for 30 seconds, until fragrant. Add the tomatoes with the juice from the can. If you're using whole tomatoes, use a potato masher to gently crush them. Add the chicken broth, basil and sugar, and bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low, cover and simmer for 15 minutes. *While your soup is simmering, make your grilled cheese sandwiches (see recipe below).
  3. Using an immersion blender or regular blender, puree the soup until you reach the desired consistency. When using a blender, work in batches and do not fill the blender more than halfway.
  4. Return the puree to the pan over low heat. Add the heavy cream and parmesan cheese and simmer until the cheese melts. Season to taste with salt and black pepper. If necessary, adjust the acidity with more sugar.
  5. Ladle the soup into bowls and top with fresh basil and more parmesan cheese and serve with a grilled cheese sandwich.
